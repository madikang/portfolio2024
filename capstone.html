Capstone

As the Graphic Designer at YogaSix, I create print and digital campaign materials across various platforms. From designing content for social media and email blasts to producing retail and development projects, I bring to life the YogaSix brand through promotions, local events, and challenge campaigns. Working in the Marketing team under our Chief Marketing Officer, I lead visual direction of national monthly marketing campaigns. I also communicate directly with clients at YogaSix studios nationwide to provide on-brand collateral for local ads, signage, event promotions, and more. Internally, I support the YogaSix team as a whole by managing and producing presentations, training modules and material, and file organization.
